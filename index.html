Yiwei “Wayne” Ge

Ph.D. Candidate in Mathematics, Louisiana State University
Advisor: James Oxley

I work in structural and extremal combinatorics, focusing on graph theory and matroid theory. My research studies connectivity, extremal density, and unavoidable substructures in graphs and matroids, with additional interest in Ramsey-theoretic methods.

Research

  • Structural and extremal graph theory
  • Matroid theory and connectivity
  • Super-minimally connected structures
  • Ramsey-type problems
